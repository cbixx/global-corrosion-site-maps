Methodology

Corrosion Atlas organizes corrosion exposure-site records from published papers, reports, datasets, and exposure-programme documents. Records may include site labels, coordinates, country or location, environmental category, metallic material, exposure period, corrosion-rate values, and source links.

The database uses harmonized site and source metadata to support comparison across exposure programmes, regions, materials, and environments. Source traceability is retained so users can verify critical values against the original documents.
