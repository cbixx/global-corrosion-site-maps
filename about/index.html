About Corrosion Atlas

Corrosion Atlas is an academic data-curation and mapping platform for corrosion exposure-site information. It is designed to connect site locations, environmental classifications, metallic materials, exposure periods, corrosion-rate records, and source evidence in a single searchable interface.

The platform is currently under active development. More information about the project background, maintainers, data scope, and collaboration channels will be added as the database matures.
